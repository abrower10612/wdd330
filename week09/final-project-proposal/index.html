Overview

The purpose of Forekala is to provide an efficient and effective way for people to keep track of their finances. Whether they're at home or on the go, Forekala will allow them to simply open the app, create a transaction and determine which category of their budget it belongs in. Forekala will allow them to see month-to-month spending and will also let them keep track of budget categories in real time.

Audience

The audience of Forekala will consist of individuals and families who are wanting to get a better grip on their finances. Most of the Forekala audience will be younger families with children that are working to make ends meet. The audience will consist of couples who maybe haven't had the easiest time being unified in their finances and are wanting an app that will help them get on the same page and stay there.
